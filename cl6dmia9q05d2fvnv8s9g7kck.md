### What are Fungible vs. non-fungible tokens?

Fungibility refers to an asset‚Äôs ability to be exchanged with a similar asset without sacrificing its value, meaning all the currency‚Äôs units (i.e., tokens) are the same and equal, like the dollar or a vag of salt.

Non-fungible tokens are the opposite‚Ää‚Äî‚Ääevery cryptocurrency unit, or token, is unique and cannot be replicated.

### **How do NFTs¬†work?**

Non-fungible tokens can be created on contract-enabled blockchains with non-fungible token tools and support. Ethereum was the first to be widely used, NEO, EOS, etc now also have NFT standards.
